## Ingredients

- 12 oz medium pasta (lumache, conchiglie, or similar)
- Kosher salt
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 white or yellow onion, finely chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- Freshly ground black pepper
- 1 small zucchini, quartered lengthwise, seeds removed, cut into ½" pieces
- 1 bunch asparagus, woody ends trimmed, thinly sliced
- 1 cup frozen broccoli florets
- 1 cup frozen peas
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 2 oz Parmesan, finely grated (~1 cup)
- ¼ cup torn fresh basil leaves, plus more for serving
- 2 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 2 Tbsp unsalted butter

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until very al dente, about 2 minutes less than package directions. Drain, reserving about 1 cup pasta cooking liquid.
2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a medium Dutch oven over medium-low. Add onion, garlic, a large pinch of salt and pepper. Cook, stirring often, until onion is softened and translucent, 8–10 minutes.
3. Add zucchini and cook, stirring often, until tender, about 5 minutes.
4. Add asparagus, frozen broccoli, frozen peas, heavy cream, cooked pasta, and ½ cup of the reserved pasta water. Cook, stirring often, until asparagus is tender and cream clings to pasta and vegetables, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
5. Add Parmesan, basil, lemon juice, and butter. Stir vigorously, adding more pasta water as needed, until cheese is melted and sauce is smooth and glossy.
6. Divide among bowls and top with additional fresh basil.
