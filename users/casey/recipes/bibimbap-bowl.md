## Ingredients

- 4 cups cooked jasmine rice (1⅓ cups uncooked)
- ½ Tbsp cooking oil
- 6 cups fresh spinach (loosely packed, ~7.5 oz)
- 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
- ¼ tsp salt
- ½ lb ground beef
- 2 Tbsp chili garlic sauce
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp brown sugar
- 1 carrot, peeled and grated
- 1 English cucumber, thinly sliced
- 2 scallions, sliced
- 4 large eggs
- ¼ cup kimchi
- 1 Tbsp sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. If rice isn't already cooked, start it first. Prepare bowl ingredients as rice cooks.
2. Heat a large skillet over medium. Add cooking oil, then spinach. Sauté until wilted, a few minutes. Drizzle sesame oil over top, season with salt, and remove to a bowl.
3. Add ground beef to the same skillet. Cook until browned, drain excess grease.
4. Add chili garlic sauce, soy sauce, and brown sugar. Stir and cook 1 minute until beef is evenly coated. Turn off heat.
5. Grate carrot, slice cucumber and scallions.
6. Fry or soft-boil 4 eggs (cook eggs fresh if meal-prepping ahead).
7. Build bowls: 1 cup rice, then divide spinach, beef, cucumber, carrots, egg, and kimchi evenly. Top with scallions and sesame seeds.
