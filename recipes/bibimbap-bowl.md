## Ingredients

- 4 cups cooked jasmine rice (about 1⅓ cups uncooked)
- ½ tbsp cooking oil
- 6 cups fresh spinach, loosely packed
- 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
- ¼ tsp salt
- ½ lb ground beef
- 2 tbsp chili garlic sauce
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 carrot, peeled and grated
- 1 English cucumber, thinly sliced
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 4 large eggs
- ¼ cup kimchi
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Cook jasmine rice if not already done. Prepare all other ingredients while rice cooks.
2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat with cooking oil. Sauté spinach until wilted. Drizzle with sesame oil, season with salt, and transfer to a bowl.
3. Brown ground beef in the same skillet, draining excess grease. Add chili garlic sauce, soy sauce, and brown sugar. Stir and cook 1 minute until beef is evenly coated.
4. Prepare fresh vegetables: grate carrot, slice cucumber, slice green onions.
5. Fry or soft-boil eggs (cook fresh per serving if meal prepping ahead).
6. Build bowls: 1 cup rice base, then divide spinach, beef, cucumber, carrot, and kimchi. Top with egg, green onions, and sesame seeds.
