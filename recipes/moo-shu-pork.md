Classic Chinese-American stir-fry with pork, egg, cabbage, and mushrooms, served in thin moo shu pancakes with hoisin sauce.

## Ingredients

### Pork marinade
- 12 oz boneless pork shoulder, partially frozen and julienned
- 1 tbsp water
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce (or soy sauce)
- 1 tsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp neutral oil

### Moo shu sauce (table sauce)
- 3 tbsp hoisin sauce
- 2 tsp hot water
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- ¼ tsp sesame oil

### Filling seasoning
- ¼ cup hot water
- 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- ½ tsp cornstarch
- ½ tsp dark soy sauce
- ⅛ tsp white pepper

### Stir-fry
- 4 tbsp neutral oil, divided
- 2 large eggs, beaten
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 3 cups Taiwanese flat cabbage or green cabbage, finely shredded
- 1½ cups julienned carrots (140g)
- 4 dried shiitake mushrooms, soaked and sliced (~½ cup / 80g rehydrated)
- 0.3 oz dried wood ear mushrooms, soaked and roughly chopped (~½ cup / 65g rehydrated)
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 2 scallions, julienned

### For serving
- 1 batch moo shu pancakes

## Instructions

1. Combine pork with marinade ingredients (water, oyster sauce, cornstarch, oil) and set aside for 15 minutes.
2. Mix the table sauce (hoisin, hot water, soy sauce, sesame oil). Microwave 20 seconds and set aside.
3. Combine filling seasoning ingredients in a small bowl.
4. Heat wok until just smoking. Add 2 tbsp oil. Pour in beaten eggs, scramble quickly, break into pieces, and remove to a plate.
5. Add 1 tbsp oil to wok. Sear pork until ~80% done, then remove.
6. Add remaining 1 tbsp oil on medium-high. Stir-fry garlic, cabbage, carrots, and mushrooms for 1 minute. Add Shaoxing wine and stir-fry 30 more seconds.
7. Add filling seasoning and cooked pork. Stir-fry 1–2 minutes. Add egg and scallions, stir 30 seconds until vegetables are wilted and most liquid has cooked off. (If too saucy, add a small cornstarch slurry to thicken.)
8. Serve immediately with moo shu pancakes and table hoisin sauce.
