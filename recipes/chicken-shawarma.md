## Ingredients

- 2 large chicken breasts (1.25 lbs)
- ½ cup plain Greek yogurt (marinade)
- 1 Tbsp lemon juice
- 1 Tbsp garlic (minced, 3 cloves)
- ½ tsp ground cinnamon
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
- ¼ tsp ground cloves
- ¾ cup plain Greek yogurt (sauce)
- ¼ tsp garlic (minced, 1 clove)
- ¼ tsp dried dill
- ¼ tsp salt
- ½ head romaine lettuce (washed, dried, chopped)
- ½ medium cucumber (sliced)
- 2 medium Roma tomatoes (sliced)
- ¼ red onion (sliced)
- 4 naan or pita or flatbread

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, combine marinade ingredients: ½ cup Greek yogurt, lemon juice, minced garlic, cinnamon, oregano, salt, nutmeg, and cloves. Stir to combine.

2. Slice chicken breasts into 1-inch-thick strips. Add to a gallon ziptop bag or mixing bowl. Pour in marinade and toss to coat well. Cover and refrigerate 4–24 hours.

3. While marinating, prepare yogurt sauce: combine ¾ cup Greek yogurt, minced garlic, dill, and salt. Cover and refrigerate.

4. Preheat grill. After marinating, grill chicken until cooked through (flip after 7 minutes). Check internal temp reaches 160°F; it will reach safe 165°F after resting covered in foil for a few minutes.

5. To serve, spread yogurt sauce on flatbread, top with chicken and fresh vegetables, roll closed. Wrap in foil or parchment if needed.
