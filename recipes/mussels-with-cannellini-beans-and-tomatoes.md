## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
- 1 tablespoon tomato paste
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 (15 ounce) can diced or crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup dry white wine
- 1 (15 ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
- 2 pounds mussels, cleaned and debearded
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Fresh parsley, roughly chopped, for garnish
- Flaky salt, for garnish
- Crusty bread, grilled or toasted, for serving

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet with a lid, heat the butter and olive oil over medium. Add the tomato paste and garlic, stir, and let cook for 2 minutes. Add the tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, and cook for 5 more minutes.
2. Add the wine, beans, and mussels, cover, and let cook until mussels have opened, about 5 minutes. Discard any mussels that did not open.
3. Garnish with fresh parsley and flaky sea salt. Serve immediately with crusty bread.
