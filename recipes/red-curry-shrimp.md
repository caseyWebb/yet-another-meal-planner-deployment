title: Red Curry Shrimp
---
Fast Thai-style red curry with shrimp and bell peppers in a light coconut broth. Ready in 20 minutes — quick enough for a weeknight.

## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp oil
- 1½ Tbsp red curry paste
- ½ small red bell pepper, cut into thick strips
- ½ small yellow bell pepper, cut into thick strips
- 12.35 oz shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 cup water
- 1½ Tbsp fish sauce (or to taste)
- 2 tsp sugar (or to taste)
- ¼ cup coconut cream
- 3 Thai red chilies, slightly bruised (optional)
- Cilantro leaves, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add red curry paste and stir-fry until fragrant.
2. Add bell peppers and stir to combine, then add shrimp and stir until evenly coated.
3. Add water, fish sauce, and sugar. Bring to a boil and cook 2–3 minutes until shrimp are cooked through.
4. Lower heat and stir in coconut cream. Bring to a gentle boil.
5. Add Thai red chilies. Dish out, garnish with cilantro, and serve immediately with steamed rice.
